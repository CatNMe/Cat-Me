About Cat&Me

About Cat&Me

Cat&Me is a friendly, easy-to-read cat-care library made for everyday cat owners.

Cat&Me was created for one simple reason: caring for a cat shouldn’t mean wading through confusing or contradictory advice. We turn common cat questions into clear, practical articles that any owner can understand — whether you’ve just brought home your first kitten or you’ve shared your life with cats for years.

What we cover

Our growing library spans the topics that matter most in day-to-day cat ownership:

  • Health & wellness — recognising illness, common conditions, and preventive care.
  • Food & nutrition — balanced feeding, safe foods, and portion guidance.
  • Behaviour & body language — understanding what your cat is telling you.
  • Kitten care, indoor enrichment, safety, everyday care, breeds, and fun facts.

Our approach

Every article is written in plain English and grounded in widely accepted, well-researched cat-care guidance. We focus on being genuinely helpful, calm, and honest — including telling you when something is a job for your vet rather than a website.

Important: Cat&Me provides general educational information only. It is not a substitute for professional veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you are worried about your cat’s health, always contact your vet.

Who we are

We’re a small team of cat lovers who believe good information helps cats live healthier, happier lives. Cat&Me is an independent publication and is not affiliated with any veterinary practice or brand.
